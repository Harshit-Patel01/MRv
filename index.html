About the Project

MRv (Movie Reviewed) is a movie recommendation platform that helps you discover the best movies based on your preferences. Our platform uses data from TMDB (The Movie Database) to provide you with accurate and up-to-date information about movies.

Our goal is to make movie discovery fun and easy for everyone.

Features

  • Movie recommendations based on genres
  • Trending movies updated daily
  • Detailed movie information
  • Beautiful and intuitive user interface
  • Mobile-friendly design
